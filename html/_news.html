Buddish // Zen for the Modern World

Buddish is a deck of cards made up of philosophies, tools, perspectives, and practices influenced by Buddhist tradition, made easily digestible, through contemporary context. Vetted by experts in theology, each card comprises a teaching that can help us uncover our best selves in the face of life’s most fierce provocations. Ancient practices have been translated, conceptually and linguistically, into Western contexts. With a card for every week of the year, through a simple, conscious, and enjoyable exercise, this artful deck promotes self- and social-improvement—zen—for the modern world. Product coming soon.
